The NDVI value ranges from -1 to 1 and shows the vigor of the crop:

Values close to 1: the more intense the green, the more vigorous the vegetation and vegetation cover. We must consider whether we are working with extensive or intensive farming or if there is bare soil since the index will consider all this. It will also measure the vigor of the underbrush.
Values close to 0: correspond to areas with very little vegetation, early stages of cultivation, bare soil, or non-productive areas.
Negative values: usually associated with areas of water, snow, or clouds. ^VU48GQZZ
